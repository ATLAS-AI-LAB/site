    About ATLAS Research Lab

    Welcome to the ATLAS - AI Trust, Learning, Architecture, and Security research lab, led by Prof. Avi Mendelson from the Technion, faculty of Computer Science. Our research lab is dedicated to advancing knowledge in the areas of AI, trust, learning, architecture, and security. We strive to make groundbreaking contributions to these fields through rigorous research and collaboration.

    Our team is composed of dedicated professionals committed to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and innovation. We are passionate about our work and driven by a common goal of making a positive impact on society through our research. Together, we explore new frontiers, develop cutting-edge solutions, and pave the way for a more secure and trustworthy digital future.
